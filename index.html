About Us

Hicswa Community Microscopy is a volunteer-run initiative founded by Koen Hicswa. We build affordable, open-source microscopes, publish detailed guides, and sponsor local STEM programs (including student robotics teams) to put advanced science tools into every classroom.

Governance

Name: Hicswa Community Microscopy

Purpose: Promote science accessibility via community-built microscopes; publish open-source educational materials; support local STEM education and robotics.

Membership: Open to anyone interested. No dues currently.

Leadership: Founder & Organizer: Koen Hicswa. Additional roles may be added as needed.

Meetings: Held as needed (virtual or in-person).

Finances: Unincorporated association. May accept donations or make charitable contributions.

Dissolution: Remaining assets to a nonprofit educational org or local STEM program (e.g., school robotics team).
