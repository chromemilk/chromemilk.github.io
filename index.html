Science, Shared and Simplified.

We build affordable, educational tools for students and hobbyists. From scanning microscopes to chemical simulation engines, we believe technology should be accessible to every curious mind.

Accessibility

Scientific equipment shouldn't cost thousands. We design DIY microscopes, software, and electrical builds that run on standard hardware and fit a student budget.

Education

We provide comprehensive documentation, buy-lists, and links to ensure anyone can replicate our builds and learn the engineering behind them.

Community

We operate as an open collective. We share knowledge freely and support our local robotics team with donations and student involvement.
