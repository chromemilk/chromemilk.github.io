Governance

Name: Hicswa Community Science

Purpose: Promote science accessibility via community-built microscopes; publish open-source educational materials; support local STEM education and robotics.

Membership: Open to anyone interested. No dues currently. Membership is automatic upon request and includes consent for names to be publicly listed on this website to acknowledge participation.

Leadership: Founders: Koen Hicswa and Kaleb Wilcox. Koen Hicswa currently serves as Organizer. Additional roles or officers may be created and filled as needed by mutual agreement of the founders or by member consensus.

Meetings: Held as needed (virtual or in-person).

Finances: Operates as an unincorporated association. The association is not a separate legal entity; any funds received (including donations) are held and managed by the organizer on behalf of the group and used solely to further its stated purpose. The association may accept voluntary contributions and may make charitable donations to support local STEM or educational programs.

Dissolution: Remaining assets to a nonprofit educational org or local STEM program (e.g., school robotics team).
