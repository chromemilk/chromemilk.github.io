Supporting Future Engineers

We are proud to support local STEM initiatives, including the FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Dayton, Ohio.

FRC Team 6032 - Pirate Robotics

West Carrollton HS Est. 2016 Dayton, OH

Pirate Robotics is a dynamic FIRST Robotics Competition team. The students design, build, and program 120lb robots to compete in annual challenges that require creativity, teamwork, and technical skill.

Their work embodies the spirit of STEM by combining mechanical engineering, electronics, and software development.
